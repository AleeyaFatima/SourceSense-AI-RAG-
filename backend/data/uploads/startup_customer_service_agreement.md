# Customer Service Level Agreement (SLA)

This document details the Service Level Agreement (SLA) guidelines and ticket resolution times for operational support.

## 1. Ticket Classification & Support Priorities
Incoming support inquiries are cataloged into three priority levels:
- **Priority 1 (Critical)**: Operational outage of primary services, databases, or key APIs affecting more than 20% of active users.
- **Priority 2 (Major)**: Partial degradation of system performance, slower RAG queries, or document upload ingestion failures.
- **Priority 3 (Minor)**: Minor dashboard UI layout alignment issues, typos in documentation, or non-blocking settings edits.

## 2. SLA Resolution Times
SourceSense commits to resolving issues within the following intervals:
- **Priority 1 (Critical)**: Support teams must initiate analysis within 15 minutes, and achieve resolution within 4 hours.
- **Priority 2 (Major)**: Support teams must initiate analysis within 2 hours, and achieve resolution within 12 hours.
- **Priority 3 (Minor)**: Support teams must resolve the ticket within 48 hours.

## 3. Escalation Pathways
If a Priority 1 issue is not resolved within 2 hours, it is escalated automatically to the Lead Operations Architect. If unresolved within 3 hours, the Chief Technology Officer (CTO) is paged.
